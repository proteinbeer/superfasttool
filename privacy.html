Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Overview

Super Fast Tool provides browser-based calculators, converters, generators, media utilities, and small games. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the website and the choices available to you.

No account or registration is required. We do not intentionally collect the values entered into calculators or generators.

Local browser processing

Most calculations and supported file operations run locally in your browser. Files selected for image, audio, or video tools are generally processed on your device and are not uploaded to a Super Fast Tool server.

You remain responsible for the files and information you choose to process. Avoid entering confidential, regulated, or sensitive information unless you are comfortable handling it in your browser environment.

Browser storage

The site may use local storage or similar browser features to remember preferences such as starred tools, selected categories, search state, pagination, and game progress. This information stays in your browser unless you clear site data or browser storage.

Contact form

When you submit the Contact form, the name, email address, and message you provide are sent to Formspree so the message can be delivered to us. Formspree processes that information under its own privacy terms. Do not include passwords, payment details, government identifiers, medical records, or other sensitive information in a message.

Advertising and cookies

Super Fast Tool may use Google AdSense or related Google advertising services. Google and its partners may use cookies, device identifiers, IP addresses, browser information, and activity data to provide, measure, personalize, or limit advertising, subject to applicable consent requirements and Google policies.

You can review how Google uses information from sites that use its services on Google's partner sites policy page. Browser settings and Google advertising controls may provide additional cookie and personalization choices.

Third-party services

The website may load resources or functionality from hosting providers, content delivery networks, translation services, styling libraries, analytics providers, advertising platforms, and form-processing services. These providers may receive standard technical information needed to deliver a request, including IP address, browser type, device details, language, referring page, requested URL, and timestamps.

Third-party services operate under their own terms and privacy policies. Super Fast Tool does not control their independent data practices.

Hosting and technical logs

Our hosting and domain infrastructure may automatically process ordinary server or security logs. These logs can include IP addresses, requested pages, timestamps, response codes, and browser or network information. Such data may be used to deliver the site, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, and maintain reliability.

Children's privacy

Super Fast Tool is intended as a general-purpose utility website and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Contact form, contact us so we can review the request.

Your choices

  • Clear local storage, cookies, or other site data through your browser settings.
  • Use browser privacy controls or extensions to limit cookies and third-party requests.
  • Choose not to submit the Contact form.
  • Contact us to ask about a message you previously submitted.

Policy updates

We may update this policy when tools, advertising, analytics, hosting, legal requirements, or third-party services change. The latest revision date appears at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Super Fast Tool contact form.
