About

Super Fast Tool is a collection of quick calculators and utility tools for everyday tasks.

It is designed for fast answers without signup, installation, or complicated menus.

All calculator inputs are processed in your browser. The site does not send your calculation data to a server.

Privacy Policy

Super Fast Tool does not require accounts and does not collect calculator input values.

Some external services may be loaded for site functionality, such as translation, styling, icons, or future advertising. These services may use their own cookies or request logs according to their policies.

If advertising is added in the future, this policy will be updated to explain ad-related cookies and choices.

Local data

Starred tools are saved only in your browser using local storage, so your favorites can stay available after a refresh.

Contact form

The contact form opens your email app. Messages are not submitted to a Super Fast Tool server.

No financial advice

Finance-related calculators are provided for quick estimates only and should not be treated as investment, tax, or legal advice.

Updates

This policy may be updated as new tools, analytics, advertising, or hosting features are added.
